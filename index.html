US Study Tour – Report On the outset I would like you thank the management for giving me an opportunity to visit to the US and take up the study tour along with Nikhil sir Following are the details about by study tour along with some of my observations and learnings: Day 1: 23rd April 2026 – Thursday I reached San Franciso, and spent the evening just exploring the neighborhood nearby hotel i.e. Holiday Inn, SFO Day 2: 24th April 2026 – Friday We visited Google office, we got to see a unique work culture and wellness of staff members We visited Computer History Museum; it had lot of information on how Computer started and which all companies and persons were responsible for various inventio and breakthrough in this field Day 3: 25th April 2026 – Saturday We had a free day and explored SFO Day 4: 26th April 2026 – Sunday We moved from SFO to Sunnyvale and spent 2nd of day in Apple Experience Centre and took a glimpse of Apple Warship Office campus, an virtual view through the technology Day 5: 27th April 2026 – Monday We had a free day and explored SFO along with our team members Day 6: 28th April 2026 – Tuesday We first visited Argonaut Elementary school (Pre KG to Grade 5) Met Principal and District Superintendent who took us around the campus and explained their system in which we saw students are given lot of exposure to become self-learners, they are exposed to all types of fields like gardening, projects which can give them real time idea on its working, fund raising, etc Importance is given to Physical Education and Music as well to a great extend We met some of the students of Grade 4 and 5 who ever very good in expressing themselves and confidently, emphasis is given on overall growth rather than marks and rote learning The same day we visited Redwood middle school (Grade 6-8), the principal took us through a presentation and explained. Learning here was to see the types of electives they use to allow students to take and explore themselves, class timings were unique and kids had a few minutes in between every class for shifting from one classroom to other Another good thought what we observed was, late comers were given a unique punishment is not to socialize during break and to sit separately and eat, this type of imposition can motivate them to be on time to school as they would not like to miss their socializing time with their peers. The quality of food at the campus was very good and we had opportunity to have our lunch here Followed by this we had an interactive session with the district leadership team where we had a good round of Q & A with each other to under both our systems better and take the good practices Overall, our learning from elementary and middle school was to make kids self-learners and independent. Create an environment where in they learn more things with hands on experience Also, unique thing here is the breaks given by teacher in the class in between teaching so that students can discuss then work on the concepts taught before going further to the next topic, this also helps to keep students concentration span intact in the class Session with Neru Khosla and team at CK12 CK12 is an online platform available for teachers and students, which will surely increasing the efficiency of the way in which lesson plans is made and micro level analysis of the student learning and activity which will in turn help the teacher to help the student accordingly This platform is totally free as of now and has lot of features better than many paid platforms as well We would like to deep dive into this platform as a pilot of some our teachers and students Day 7: 29th April 2026 – Wednesday We started our day with visit to Oracle Design Tech High School (Grade 9 – 12), met the co-founder of the school, who took us through a presentation and also connected us to some of their students. Here much concentration is given on project based learning along with their regular school learning process, students are exposed to various fields of Design Thinking almost in all fields some or the other student is working on, they also have classes from employees of Oracle who would guide and train them in their ideas of Design Thinking, unique thing to see is no one spoon fed here but only guided and given freedom to take it forward Students here also much involved in making their good profile for their desired admissions in the Universities. We then visited Stanford University; we got chance to visit the School of Education and to the AI team to understand who they implement it in university learning We could overall go around the entire campus and get a feel of the legacy of this university Day 8: 30th April 2026 – Thursday Our entire day was planned to be spent in Saratoga High School (Grade 9-12), we here met the Principal and his team Students here are also allowed to choose from a wide range of electives, major concentration was on application based learning, its more student centric and parents are consciously involved and not casually in the school activities. Music and Physical education is given good importance, engineering lab with all types of facilities like 3D printers also available Students get to choose STEM branch wise subject wise and standard of testing can vary and also credits can continue upto University as well Highlight here was the environment created in Wellness room of the students and career counselling room for the students, in these way every student in campus is given importance and no one will feel left out or inferior We had lunch with students here and some of them are already doing some very good projects which shows their care and empathy towards the society and its issues. Students’ confidence reflected their learning happening in the right manner Our day concluded with an insightful session with Barry Groves, President of WASC (Western Association of Schools and Colleges), it was very insightful and also learnt its importance across globe for school which follows international curriculum like IB or IGCSE Day 9: 1st May 2026 – Friday Free day, we explored Mystery Spot and 17th Mile Drive Few of the points which I look forward in implementing here: 1.
